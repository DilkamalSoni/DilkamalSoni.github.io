03
Completed — 2022
Photolithography MATLAB Precision fabrication Signal integrity

Smart Contact Lens Pressure Sensors

Hong Kong University of Science and Technology

Fabricated and tested wireless intraocular pressure (IOP) sensors embedded in contact lenses for early diagnosis of ocular disease. Sensors produced via photolithography, mould casting, and chemical etching. Signal integrity verified using a Vector Network Analyser (VNA); data captured and processed in MATLAB. The precision fabrication process required maintaining micron-level tolerances across a high-volume batch — directly analogous to the tight manufacturing tolerances required in electric motor winding and lamination assembly.

  • 92 samples fabricated and validated for in-vivo hospital testing
  • 40% improvement in fabrication efficiency through process optimisation
  • VNA signal integrity validation across full sample batch
  • Samples submitted to hospital authorities for clinical evaluation
